🌿 Numerology Guide
🌍 What is Numerology?
Numerology is the ancient study of how numbers shape our lives, personalities, and destinies. Rooted in Pythagorean philosophy, it reveals that your birth date and name carry a unique cosmic code — each digit vibrating with special energy and meaning that guides your path.
🌟 Life Path Number
The most important number in your chart. It reveals your life's purpose, the path you're meant to walk, and the core lessons you're here to learn. Think of it as your soul's GPS for this lifetime — always pointing toward your truest direction.
📅 Month + Day + Year → reduce to 1–9
🎯 Destiny (Expression) Number
Reveals your natural talents, abilities, and what you are truly destined to achieve in this life. Every letter of your full birth name is assigned a Pythagorean value and summed together, then reduced.
📝 A=1, B=2, C=3 … all letters → reduce
💭 Soul Urge Number
The voice of your innermost self — your heart's most secret desires and what truly brings deep joy to your soul. Only the vowels in your full birth name are used, making this the most intimate of all your numbers.
💖 Vowels only (A E I O U) → reduce
🎭 Personality Number
How the world perceives you before they truly know you. Your outer persona and the very first impression you create. This number is built only from the consonants in your birth name.
👥 Consonants only → reduce
🔢 How to Reduce Numbers
Keep adding all digits together until you reach a single digit (1–9). For example: Birthday 29/11/1995 → 2+9+1+1+1+9+9+5 = 37 → 3+7 = 10 → 1+0 = 1. Important exception: Master Numbers 11, 22, and 33 are never reduced further — they carry extra power.
⚡ Master Numbers — Extra Power
These three special numbers carry amplified cosmic energy. If you calculate one of these at any stage of reduction, stop — do not reduce further.
11
The Illuminator
Highly intuitive, spiritual visionary, a channel for higher wisdom
22
The Master Builder
Turns the greatest dreams into enduring reality on a grand scale
33
The Master Teacher
Rare selfless healer dedicated to uplifting all of humanity

🌸 About Cosmic Numbers
🌟 What is Cosmic Numbers?
Cosmic Numbers is your personal numerology companion — a free, beautiful tool that decodes the hidden language of your birth date and name. We blend ancient Pythagorean numerology with a joyful, natural experience so every soul can discover their cosmic story.
🔢 Our Formula
We use the classical Pythagorean system — the most widely practised numerology method worldwide. Each letter maps to a number 1–9, all calculations reduce to a single digit, and Master Numbers 11, 22 and 33 are preserved in their full power.
🔒 Your Privacy
Everything is calculated entirely inside your browser. We never store, transmit or share your name or birth date. Your cosmic data belongs only to you — zero tracking, zero servers, pure local magic.
